Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?
I offer home-based beauty services like facials, clean-ups, waxing, threading, hair spa, and more. Feel free to ask for custom combos! 💆‍♀️💅
Do I need to bring anything?
Nope! I bring all the necessary products and tools. You just relax and enjoy your pampering time at home 😊
How can I book an appointment?
You can call or WhatsApp me, or book through the website. Choose your service and preferred time — I’ll confirm everything personally 📅📞

Absolutely! I use branded, skin-friendly products and follow proper hygiene with clean tools and sanitized kits 🧼🧴
